#### **A SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST**



##### ✅ ***Do’s***



* Think before you post—once it’s online, it stays.
* Use privacy settings to control who sees your content.
* Be respectful in comments and messages.
* Share useful and positive content.
* Keep your profile professional (especially LinkedIn).









##### ❌ ***Don’ts***



* Don’t share personal details like phone number or address.
* Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
* Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links.
* Don’t spread fake news without verifying it.
* Don’t argue or fight publicly online.

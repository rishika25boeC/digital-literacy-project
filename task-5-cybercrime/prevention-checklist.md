#### **Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**







##### ***✅Checklist :***

&#x20;

• I try to keep strong passwords and avoid using the same one everywhere. 

• I always turn on 2-factor authentication for important apps like email and banking. 

• I avoid clicking on random links that come on WhatsApp or SMS, especially if they look urgent or “too good to be true.” 

• Before applying for any job or internship online, I make sure it’s from a genuine source. 

• I keep my phone and apps updated so that security issues don’t build up. 

• I avoid using public Wi-Fi for things like payments or logging into important accounts. 

• I never share my UPI PIN with anyone, no matter what they say. 

• I remind myself that I don’t need to enter a PIN to receive money. 

• I log out properly if I ever use someone else’s device. 

• I try to keep a backup of important files, just in case something goes wrong.

**UPI Safety**:
Be wary of small transactions from unknown sources; they might be test transactions to catch you.

**UPI Safety**: 
"Scan to receive money" is a red flag; money transfer needs scanning, not receiving
